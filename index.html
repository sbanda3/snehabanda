Developer of App, SunScan @ Google Play  Aug 2023 —Present

Currently working on publishing an app to help contribute to sun health education regarding UV Index scores and Rays in order to expand on my UV sensor research project! My UV index app is a mobile application which provides users real-time UV index information for a specific location. It allows users to input their location, and the app retrieves and displays the current UV index level. Based on the UV index, the app offers personalized advice and recommendations for sun protection measures. This advice can include suggestions for sunscreen application, protective clothing, sunglasses, and other precautions to help users minimize their exposure to harmful ultraviolet (UV) radiation. The app is a valuable tool for everybody, as protecting people's skin from the potential risks associated with excessive sun exposure is important for anybody that spends time outside.
